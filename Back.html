Back

It is the best exercise to increase the frame in upper body exercise.
You must understand the movement of the scapula well. It is one of the most difficult exercises for beginners, and it is difficult to feel the stimulation of the back.
If you take it one step at a time, one day you will grow. There are many types of exercises such as lat pulldowns, pull-ups, deadlifts, barbell rows, sited low, etc.
Among them, the exercises that beginners should do are lat pulldowns, sited low, and pull-ups. Deadlifts and barbell rows are intermediate and advanced Since it is the above exercise, it is good to start with some exercise to stimulate the muscles.
Pull-ups are a very difficult exercise for first-timers, so it is better to do them using a band.
The back is a good area to develop overall upper body strength.
