Chest

That doesn't mean you can't feel masculine in other parts, though. There are many chest exercises such as push-ups, bench presses, incline presses, and dips.
Among them, it is helpful for beginners to first develop chest muscles to some extent through push-ups and dips and then perform exercises such as bench press and incline to feel chest stimulation. If you try, you will not feel the stimulation well, and there is a risk of injury.
After doing some push-ups, you can start with the bench press, which is the flower of your chest exercise.
The bench press is the most effective exercise that stimulates all parts of the chest, so it is a good idea to include it in your exercise. And you can fill in the gaps with dips or incline press.
